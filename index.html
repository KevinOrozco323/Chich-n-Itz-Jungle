Chichén Itzá Jungle

Chichén Itzá Jungle was founded and built in 2010. Chichén Itzá Jungle is here to provide tourists with the best natural Mayan experience. Our park was built on Native land to conserve and protect existing habitats. We are located in the Northeast region close to Chichen Itza Temples.

Attractions

Off-Road/ATV

Chichen Itza Jungle offers a fun thrilling off-road experience while providing a safe place for all ages. We have ATV's and all terrain vehicles ready for use and provide safety equipment as well. Riders can take pictures while riding their vehicles and collect their photos at the gift shop.

Regular Price:$39.99(All Day) Outdoor Bundle Price: $80(ATV/Off-road and Zipline) Bundle Price: $150(ALL)

Zipline

Chichen Itza Jungle provides extreme fun and safe ziplines in the park with some connecting to our natural cenotes. The Zipline is for all ages due to Chichen Itza jungle offering adult and kid friendly ziplines.

Regular Price: $39.99 (All Day) Outdoor Bundle Price: $80 (ATV/Off-road and Zipline) Bundle Price: $150 (ALL)

Snorkeling

Chichen Itza Jungle focuses on conserving the natural resources in our native land. Our snorkeling area is surrounded by beautiful limestone rock with natural fish habitat underwater. One can enjoy the natural waters of our ancestors, swim with tropical fish,and take cool underwater photos.

Regular Price: $39.99 (All Day) Water Bundle Price: $80 (Snorkeling and Cenotes) Bundle Price: $150 (ALL)

Cenotes

Chichen Itza Jungle is dedicated to providing the most natural and beautiful cenotes. Our cenote attractions are built on top of natural tropical fish habitats covered in limestone where one can swim with the fishes.

Regular Price: $39.99 (All Day) Water Bundle Price: $80 (Snorkeling and Cenotes) Bundle Price: $150 (All)

Amenities

Our Gift shops offer a variety of different gifts ranging from a collection of precious stones to edgy merchandise. Our gift shop sells water equipment, bottled/concealed food, and many . For Every purchase made we donate 10% of proceeds to helping protect other indeginous land in the Yucatan Peninsula.

Chichen Itza Jungle has provided customers with the chance to experience what it was like to get a tribal tattoo. We offer a class where customers can learn basic Mayan words and can get Mayan inspired henna tattoos.

Chichen Itza Jungle's priority is safety for all guests in the park, all our staff are trained in safety practice and basic medical attention. We offer accommodation for disabled guests and family upon request.

Chichen Itza Jungle buffet offers a wide variety of cuisine from around the world ranging from american, mexican, italian and asain plates. Our buffet has a minibar for adults, while drinks can only be drunk inside the buffet area.
